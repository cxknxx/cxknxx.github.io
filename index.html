These are just some demos that i've recorded when ive had time.

I'm awake but my spirit's in bed

A collab between Russell and I, worked out a demo and sent it over to him to work his production magic on. Though it's not done I really love the direction it's heading in.

Acoustic and Bass

I love the main riff of this piece. Probably one of my favourite recordings that i've ever created. Really would love to use this in a song as I think that it would fit really well with some vocals.

Delay

While this is a really rough demo (And I mean like 5 minute recording rough), I loved doing the layering of guitars. The song has an 'Explosions in the Sky' vibe and it makes me want to experiment creating songs using nothing but a guitar and effects.

Acoustic double track

Found this on my pc recorded under the name "Demo 1". No idea how I played it but would love to remember because I really dig the chilled out acoustic vibe that it has going on.

Chillwave

This is a short demo of me working with chillwave style music. Overall just trying out new synth sounds.

Arp demo

This demo is mainly for use of an arpeggiator because I love using them and really wanted to incorporate them into a song somehow.
